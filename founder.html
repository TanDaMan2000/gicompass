Why this project

Why Gastro Compass matters to him

Gastro Compass is not meant to be another generic health app. The project is being built around a sharper problem: how to use AI to help surface earlier signs that could matter in digestive disease, especially when patient stories and clinical data live in separate places.

That is why the platform is organized around two sides at once: the patient view for symptom history and the professional view for colonoscopy and endoscopy uploads. The founder vision is to make those two streams work together instead of forcing patients and clinicians to bridge the gap manually.
